Mariah's Plane Log

Hi baby! I know how much you love planes, so I thought it would be fun to have a little log where you can keep track of all the planes you've seen. You can search for any flight or aircraft and add it to your log with a note about where you saw it or any special memories associated with it. I hope this makes your plane-spotting adventures even more enjoyable! I love you to the moon and back! ✈️❤️

Note: The database I used is for real-time flights only, so unfortunately, you can add sightings of planes that are still in the air but not those that have already landed 🙁. However, I will be adding a feature in the future to allow you to add past sightings as well, so stay tuned! hehe
